About My FBLA Path

My first competiton I found a love for everything business related. I felt like I truly found my passion so I jumped in head first. I decided to competer in 7 competions at region. Then 3 at State where I also ran for State Officer. I became the first person ever in my county to run for State Officer. I then placed at state and won an Officer postion. This continued my path to comepete at the National Competiton.

My FBLA Experience
